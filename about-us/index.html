About Skagit River

Redefining the meaning
of care.

We believe every individual deserves to age with dignity, surrounded by warmth, profound respect, and a community that feels genuinely like family.

Our vision
Our Vision

We envision a future where adult family homes are the premier choice for aging gracefully. A future where the stigma of elder care is entirely erased, replaced by a profound sense of community, joy, and uncompromising medical oversight.

Through continuous training of our exceptional staff, transparent communication with families, and an unyielding commitment to holistic well-being, we aim to set the absolute gold standard for residential elder care in our community and beyond.

Our mission
Our Mission

Our mission is rooted in a single, unwavering belief: that the transition into assisted living should not mean a loss of independence, identity, or comfort. We exist to provide a seamless extension of home for seniors who require assistance with daily living.

By limiting our residency size, we remove the institutional feel of traditional facilities. Instead, we foster an environment where highly personalized, compassionate care is the daily standard, ensuring that every resident feels seen, heard, and deeply valued.

How It Started

Our Journey

From a simple, heartfelt realization to the establishment of a sanctuary of care.

01

The Calling

Years spent working within the traditional healthcare system opened our eyes to a glaring gap in senior care. While large facilities provided adequate medical attention, they often lacked the warmth, personalization, and intimate human connection that individuals truly need to thrive in their later years. We witnessed the emotional toll that institutional settings took on both the elderly and their anxious families.

02

The Foundation

Driven by a desire to offer a better alternative, Skagit River was conceptualized. We sought out a beautiful, accessible residential property and transformed it completely. Every hallway, bathroom, and communal space was meticulously redesigned to meet strict accessibility and safety standards, without ever compromising on the aesthetic comfort of a true, inviting home.

03

The Present

Today, Skagit River stands as a beacon of specialized adult family care. We have built an incredible team of licensed caregivers, nurses, and support staff who treat our residents like their own family members. It is a place filled with laughter, shared meals, gentle music, and the profound peace of mind that comes from knowing your loved ones are exactly where they need to be.
