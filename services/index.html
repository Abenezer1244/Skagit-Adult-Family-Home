The Foundation

Our Care
Approach

Person-Centered Focus

We don't believe in one-size-fits-all care. Every resident undergoes a comprehensive assessment to craft an individualized care plan. This living document adapts as their needs change, ensuring they always receive the exact level of support required to maintain dignity and independence.

Proactive Health Monitoring

Our 24/7 awake staff and licensed nursing oversight allow us to detect subtle changes in health early. By coordinating seamlessly with primary care physicians and specialists, we prevent complications and keep families fully informed every step of the way.

"Our goal is never to take over a resident's life, but to provide the gentle scaffolding they need to continue living it beautifully."

What We Provide

Our Core Services

Comprehensive daily support designed to remove the burden of chores and medical management, allowing residents to focus on living.

Activities of Daily Living

Discreet, respectful assistance with bathing, dressing, grooming, and personal hygiene. We honor personal routines and preferences above all.

Medication Management

Strict RN-delegated administration of all medications. We handle refills, pharmacy coordination, and track schedules meticulously.

Culinary & Nutrition

Three delicious, home-cooked meals daily, plus snacks. We accommodate complex dietary restrictions, pureed diets, and diabetic meal plans.

Mobility & Transfer

Expert assistance with ambulation, standing, and bed-to-chair transfers using safe, state-of-the-art techniques and equipment.

Housekeeping & Laundry

Complete management of the household environment. Daily room tidying, thorough cleaning, and personal laundry services.

Engagement & Activities

Cognitive stimulation and social engagement through music, art therapy, light exercise, gardening, and family gatherings.

Our Specialties

Beyond standard assisted living, our staff is extensively trained to handle complex conditions requiring a higher, more nuanced level of care.

Memory Care

Dedicated support for Alzheimer's and various stages of dementia. We utilize validation therapy, secure environments, and familiar routines to reduce anxiety and promote peace of mind.

  • Wandering prevention
  • Behavioral redirection

Hospice Support

We partner seamlessly with local hospice agencies to provide compassionate end-of-life care in the comfort of our home, ensuring absolute dignity, pain management, and emotional support.

  • 24/7 comfort monitoring
  • Family counseling support

Respite Care

Short-term stays designed to give primary caregivers a necessary rest, or to provide a supportive environment for seniors recovering from surgery or hospitalization before returning home.

  • Flexible stay durations
  • Full access to amenities

Conditions we serve

We welcome residents with a wide range of needs. Our team is experienced in supporting those with:

  • Dementia & Alzheimer's
  • Stroke recovery
  • COPD & respiratory care
  • Mobility issues & fall risk
  • Hospice & end-of-life care
  • Diabetes & medication management
  • Post-hospitalization & respite

What's included in the base rate

Our monthly rate typically includes:

  • 24/7 supervision & awake staff
  • Three meals daily + snacks
  • Medication management (RN-delegated)
  • Assistance with ADLs (bathing, dressing, grooming)
  • Housekeeping & laundry
  • Activities & engagement
  • Care coordination with doctors & families

Some items (e.g. certain medical supplies, outside therapies, or one-on-one nursing beyond the base level) may be billed separately. We outline this clearly before admission.

Staffing & care plan process

We maintain a high staff-to-resident ratio with awake coverage 24/7. Our team includes licensed caregivers and RN oversight for assessments and medication delegation.

Before move-in we complete an assessment and create an individualized care plan. We review and update the plan regularly and whenever needs change, in partnership with families and physicians.

For exact rates and what might be included or billed separately for your situation, contact us or see our Pricing page.
